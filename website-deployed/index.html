Agent Harness for Optimization Research

Built an LLM agent harness that searches proof strategies, structures lemmas, and reasons across long mathematical arguments. The system contributed to work on an open SGD theorem; the proof and repository remain private while the paper is being prepared.

Medical AIComputer Vision

Mitral Valve Segmentation from Echocardiography

Built and trained a custom U-Net pipeline to segment mitral valves across echocardiographic video. The project combined expert and non-expert annotations, targeted augmentation, two-stage training, and IoU-based evaluation to produce frame-level predictions on unseen clinical sequences.
